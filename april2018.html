Pulitzer Series

The Center for Literary Arts celebrates National Poetry Month with Peter Balakian and President Mary Papazian at the Hammer Theatre.

Peter Balakian recently won the 2016 Pulitzer Prize for Ozone Journal which recounts the speaker’s experience in excavating bones of victims of Armenian genocide in the Syrian desert. His writing has received praise from critics and readers alike. Read more.

What Is The Hammer?
The Hammer Theatre Center is a distinctive, high-quality performance venue in the heart of downtown San José. Operated by San José State University, the theatre serves the university and the community through artistically and educationally excellent programming expressive of the unique characteristics and diverse cultures that comprise Silicon Valley.
