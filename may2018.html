National Geographic Live

National Geographic Live returns to the Hammer with photojournalist Ami Vitale! She has lived in mud huts and war zones, contracted malaria, and donned a panda suit—all in keeping with her philosophy of “living the story.” Her camera has taken her to more than 90 countries, bearing witness not only to violence and conflict but also to surreal beauty and to the enduring power of the human spirit. Kenji Williams' stunning fusion of human art, scientific visualizations, and cultural tradition guides audiences through a visceral flow of beauty and soulful data that culminates with an empowering message of care and respect for our surroundings and the planet. What Is The Hammer?
The Hammer Theatre Center is a distinctive, high-quality performance venue in the heart of downtown San José. Operated by San José State University, the theatre serves the university and the community through artistically and educationally excellent programming expressive of the unique characteristics and diverse cultures that comprise Silicon Valley.
